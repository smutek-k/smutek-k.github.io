Website of Krzysztof Smutek

I completed my PhD in Probability Theory at the University of Luxembourg, supervised by Prof. Giovanni Peccati. I am interested in probability theory with a pinch of science, specifically stochastic geometry, with the use of Malliavin-Stein techniques to tackle problems motivated by quantum physics (quantum chaos, high-frequency random waves, quantum field theory) and neuroscience (brain imaging, universal cerebellar transform).
